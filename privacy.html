Privacy Policy Updated: June 15, 2024

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use UpLift, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Discord Server Information: Server IDs, channel IDs, and role information necessary for the bot to function
  • Usage Data: Commands used, partner requests, and interaction patterns
  • Technical Information: IP addresses, device information, and browser type when accessing our website (if applicable)

1.2 Information You Provide

Some features may require you to provide information directly, such as when submitting a server for promotion or applying for a partnership. This may include:

  • Server descriptions or partnership messages
  • Contact or representative Discord usernames

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve UpLift’s services and features
  • Display and manage partnerships and server promotions
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized use
  • Respond to user inquiries and provide support
  • Monitor and analyze bot usage to maintain performance

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except in the following situations:

  • With your consent: When you authorize us to share or display your server’s promotional information
  • Service Providers: Trusted services that help us maintain and operate UpLift (e.g., hosting providers)
  • Legal Requirements: If required to comply with applicable laws or valid legal processes

4. Data Retention

We retain collected data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. Server and usage data may be deleted upon request or after inactivity.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you or your server
  • Request deletion of stored data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise these rights, please contact the UpLift team through our official support server.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

UpLift is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. If we become aware of data collected from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a revised "Effective Date." Continued use of UpLift after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us via our official support server or through other official UpLift communication channels.
