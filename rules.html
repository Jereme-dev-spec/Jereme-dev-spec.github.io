Discord Server Rules Updated: June 15, 2024

1. Respect All Members

All members must treat others with respect. Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or personal attacks are strictly prohibited.

2. No NSFW or Inappropriate Content

Explicit, sexual, violent, or otherwise inappropriate content is not allowed anywhere in the server, including usernames and profile images.

3. No Spam or Advertising

  • No unsolicited advertisements or self-promotion
  • No mass mentions, repeated messages, or disruptive behavior
  • Advertising is only allowed with explicit staff approval

4. Follow Discord Terms of Service

All members must comply with Discord’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines at all times.

5. Use Channels Properly

Keep discussions relevant to each channel’s purpose. Off-topic or disruptive content may be removed.

6. No Exploits or Abuse

Abusing bots, exploiting bugs, or attempting to bypass server systems will result in enforcement actions.

7. Staff Authority

Server staff decisions are final. Arguing moderation actions publicly may result in further penalties.

8. Punishments

Violations of these rules may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or permanent bans depending on severity.

9. Rule Updates

Rules may be updated at any time. Continued participation in the server implies acceptance of the latest version.

By participating in the UpLift Discord server, you agree to follow these rules.
